Good to know
When do I see the driving time on my balance?
After each completed lesson, with a 7-day window in case of cancellations or disputes. So expect about a week between your friend's lesson and the credit landing.
What if my friend cancels?
No problem — you only earn on lessons that actually go ahead. Cancelled or no-show lessons don't count.
Can I take the reward as cash?
Not right now — rewards are driving time only. Once you've passed your test, we're working on options like donating your earned time to other learners. More on that soon.
Is there a limit?
No cap. Refer as many friends as you like. Each one's lessons keep adding driving time to your balance.
