6. Test Ready Guarantee programme

  • The Test Ready Guarantee covers the 18-week structured programme as described on the platform.
  • To qualify for continued lessons under the guarantee, the learner must attend all scheduled sessions and follow instructor guidance throughout the programme.

  • Test Fail Cover add-ons (3rd attempt, 4th attempt, Lifetime) extend the guarantee as described at the point of purchase.
  • The Test Ready Guarantee is non-refundable once the programme has commenced.
