Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

CoachCarter Driving School ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

CoachCarter Driving School is a driving instruction business operating in the Reading area. For data protection queries, contact us at: hello@coachcarter.co.uk

2. What data we collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, email address and phone number (via our enquiry form or booking system)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Details about your driving experience or lesson preferences
  • Marketing preferences (only if you opt in)

3. How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and arrange lessons
  • Process payments for lessons and packages
  • Send booking confirmations and lesson reminders
  • Send marketing communications (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our services

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of: contract performance (to deliver lessons you have booked), legitimate interests (to respond to enquiries), and consent (for marketing communications).

5. How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations — typically no longer than 3 years after your last interaction with us.

6. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted third-party services that help us operate, including:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Setmore (booking system)
  • Resend / Nodemailer (email delivery)
  • Neon (database hosting)

All third parties are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with UK data protection law.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for marketing)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@coachcarter.co.uk.

8. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to function correctly. We do not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.

10. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
