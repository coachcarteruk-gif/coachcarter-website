The Programme

Three tiers, one journey

Each tier has a clear purpose, a pass criteria, and a transformation we can measure. You only move on when you're genuinely ready.

Tier 1 · Weeks 1–6

Road Fundamentals

By the end of Tier 1 you'll be driving independently around simple junctions and residential areas — with the safe habits baked in from day one.

~9 hrs Typical
Fast learner: 4–6 hrs
More time needed: 10–14 hrs

What You'll Learn

  • →Moving off, slowing down, and stopping safely
  • →Steering with awareness — mirrors before you move
  • →Mirror checks at the right moments
  • →The MICE junction routine (Mirrors, Indicator, Control, Evaluate)
  • →Choosing the right speed for every situation
  • →Meeting traffic and basic lane position

How You'll Know You've Passed

  • ✓Habitually moves off with appropriate checks
  • ✓Habitually slows and stops safely
  • ✓Habitually steers with mirror checks
  • ✓Habitually uses MICE correctly at junctions
  • ✓Habitually chooses the correct speed
Weekly focus
Wk 1 — Moving & Stopping Wk 2/3 — Mirrors & MICE Wk 4/5 — Junctions Wk 5/6 — Lane & Speed · Review
Tier 2 · Weeks 7–13

Road Confidence

By the end of Tier 2 you'll confidently handle all road scenarios — roundabouts, faster roads, manoeuvres — keeping yourself and others safe throughout.

~10 hrs Typical
Fast learner: 4–6 hrs
More time needed: 10–12 hrs

What You'll Learn

  • →Roundabouts — safe entry, lane discipline, and exits
  • →Faster roads (40, 50, 60mph) and following distance
  • →Reversing manoeuvres — slow, aware, and accurate
  • →Traffic lights and pedestrian crossings
  • →Crossroads and Y-junctions
  • →Spiral roundabouts and complex lane choice

How You'll Know You've Passed

  • ✓Habitually moves, steers & stops with appropriate checks
  • ✓Habitually chooses the correct speed
  • ✓Habitually keeps the car safe during reversing
  • ✓Habitually uses correct MICE at all junctions and roundabouts
  • ✓Habitually makes go/stop decisions in good time
Weekly focus
Wk 7/8 — Roundabouts & Faster Roads Wk 9/10 — Manoeuvres & Crossings Wk 10/11 — Complex Junctions · Review Wk 12/13 — Refinement
Tier 3 · Weeks 12–18

Road Independence

By the end of Tier 3 you'll drive safely without input from anyone — no habits that create risk, and mock tests passed under 3 minors.

~6 hrs Typical
Fast learner: 3–5 hrs
More time needed: 7–9 hrs

What You'll Learn

  • →Driving independently — every decision made by you
  • →Lane changing and motorway-standard discipline
  • →Parking — safe and consistently accurate
  • →Emergency stops and independent moving off
  • →Understanding and self-correcting driving faults
  • →Mock tests on real test routes

How You'll Know You're Test Ready

  • ✓Passing mock tests consistently
  • ✓Passing with under 6 minor faults
  • ✓Passing with under 3 minor faults
  • ✓No habitual faults present
  • ✓Speed, position and MICE all consistently correct
Weekly focus
Wk 12/13 — Into Tier 3 Wk 14/15 — Mock Tests Wk 16/17 — Under 6 Minors Wk 18 — Under 3 Minors · Test Ready
Week by Week

Your 18-Week Journey

Every learner moves at their own pace — this is the typical path from your first lesson to test day.

Week 1
Moving, Slowing, Stopping & Steering — the foundations everything else is built on.
Weeks 2–3
Mirror Comprehension + MICE — understanding what to check, when, and why.
Weeks 4–5
Junction Approach + Decision Making — reading the road and making confident choices.
Weeks 5–6 Tier 1 Review
Meeting Traffic + Lane Position — consolidating everything before your Tier 1 sign-off.
Weeks 7–8
Roundabouts + Faster Roads — building speed and spatial awareness in more complex environments.
Weeks 9–10
Manoeuvres + Crossings — slow-speed control, reversing, and pedestrian awareness.
Weeks 10–11 Tier 2 Review
Complex Junctions + Spiral Roundabouts — the most demanding scenarios before your Tier 2 sign-off.
Weeks 12–13
Into Tier 3 — Independent Driving — targeting individual weak points, driving without prompts.
Weeks 14–15
Mock Tests on Test Routes — real conditions, real feedback, measurable progress.
Weeks 16–17
Passing Mocks Under 6 Minors — consistency under pressure on the routes that matter.
Week 18 Test Ready
Passing Mocks Under 3 Minors — when you're consistently here, you're ready. Test booked, confidence earned.
